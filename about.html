This is Me!

I am currently a first year international student at Brandeis. Last year I took a gap year after high school, and I found an interest in coding and skateboarding. I am going to major in Computer Science. Other than coding, I play the violin and the viola. I also love to play basketball.

What happend during the gap year?

I started skateboarding during the gap year, and absolutely fell in love with it. I got my self injured and was forced to rest for 2 months, but It didn’t stop me from practicing tricks. Now skateboarding is an essential part of my life!

During the gap year I completed the mandatory military training in Taiwan. It lasted 4 months, I almost wasn’t required for the training because I have flat fleet, but I am grateful that I have the opportunity to complete the training. I met people with different backgrounds, and different jobs. We lived in a room where it has to fit 30 people in it. We only had 5 minutes to shower, and we go to sleep at 9pm wake up at 4:30am. At first I was hesitant entering the camp, I feel like it’s a waste of time, but after the training I matured a lot. It is one hell of an experience !

I also applied for jobs last year!
